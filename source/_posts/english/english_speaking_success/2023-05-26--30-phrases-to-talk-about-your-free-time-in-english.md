# Topic 
It's about what you do in your free time, or your leisure time, as they say across the pond in America. You can visit the original video link [here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0AIlXB2XNBM&t=213s)

# Goal
By the end of the vedio, we will be able to talk confidently about our hobbies, how often we do them, and why we like them. And by the way, we can also learn how to use different tenses to talk about hobbies as well.

# Content
## Different type of Free time Activities
There are probably four common types of activities:
  - indoor activities: should include playing games, board games or video games, reading, watching TV, or watching firms. or just chilling out, hanging out with friends.
  - outdoor activities: can include things like sports, be in playing football, skiing, swimming, jogging, gardening, traveling, going shopping.
  - collections: means collecting things. The most common collections are stamps, coins, and nowadays, things like NFTs.
  - creative activities: Things like playing a musical instrument, such as playing guita, painting, maybe knitting or some similar handcraft work. and also, taking photographs.
