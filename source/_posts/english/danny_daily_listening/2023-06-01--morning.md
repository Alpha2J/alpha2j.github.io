# 有字幕（精听）

Many people wonder is red <u>velvet</u> cake simply just chocolate cake with red food <u>dye</u>? And the answer is: not exactly. Though the dessert has <u>cocoa</u> <u>powder</u> as one of the <u>ingredients</u>, it also calls for <u>vinegar</u> and <u>buttermilk</u>. <u>The acidic flavors mixed with the cream cheese frosting makes for a dessert that is definitely not chocolate cake</u>. The red velvet cake <u>stems back to</u> a marketing ploy by an American food coloring company. <u>During the Great Depression</u> in order to boost sales, <u>the Adams Extract company</u> added red food coloring to velvet cake in order to give the bright distinct color that we know today. So as demand for the dessert increased, <u>so did their revenue</u>. Banoffee pie is a sweet combination of bananas, <u>toffee</u> and <u>whipped</u> cream on a <u>thick</u> graham-cracker <u>crust</u>. The word banofee itself is actually a <u>portmanteau</u> from words banana and toffee. It was invented at a restaurant in Susses, England, and became world-famous.

> 很多人想知道红色天鹅绒蛋糕是否只是添加了红色食用染料的巧克力蛋糕？答案是：不完全是。尽管这款甜点中有可可粉作为其中之一的配料，但它还需要加入醋和酪乳。酸味的调味料与奶油奶酪糖霜混合在一起，使得这款甜点绝对不是巧克力蛋糕。红色天鹅绒蛋糕的起源可以追溯到一家美国食用染料公司的营销策略。在大萧条时期，亚当斯香精公司为了提高销售额，在天鹅绒蛋糕中添加了红色食用染料，赋予了我们今天所熟知的明亮独特的颜色。因此，随着对这款甜点的需求增加，他们的收入也随之增加。香蕉太妃酥派是由香蕉、太妃糖和打发奶油放在厚厚的葛拉砂饼皮上的一种甜点组合。"Banoffee"这个词实际上是由"banana"和"toffee"两个词组合而成。它是在英国苏塞克斯的一家餐厅发明的，并且成为了世界闻名的甜点。
